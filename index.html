Postcentive For Products

Maniquens and models allow shoppers to visualize themselves wearing or using a product, but they have one major flaw. Shoppers want to see the product on somebody who matches their skin tone, body type, and personal style before making a purchasing decision. These photos already exist on Instagram, and Postcentive allows you to categorize and filter these photos to give your customers the shopping experience they deserve.
