Product Marketing

Maniquens and models allow shoppers to visualize themselves wearing or using a product, but they have a major flaw. Shoppers want to see the product on somebody who matches their body type and personal style before making a purchasing decision. These photos already exist on Instagram, and Postcentive allows you to categorize and filter these photos to give your customers the shopping experience they desire.

  • Peer reviews are more trusteed than company descriptions
  • Large gallery of real world application of products
  • Reward customers for posting about you on social media with more traffic

Event Marketing

If you are promoting an event, one of your biggest challenges is communicating an experience. Luckily, many of your fans are already sharing their experience by posting photos on Instagram. Postcentive allows you to find these photos, categorize them however you need, and remove irrelevant photos so that potential customers get to view the event from their peer's perspective instead of the promoting company.

  • Promote customer images to encourage future attendance
  • Have reliable and trusted photography from sources your audience trusts
  • Gain easy and instant access to event photos

Venue Marketing
